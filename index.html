About Seven

"707 (Real name Saeyoung Choi, Baptismal name Luciel Choi) often referred as Seven, is one of the Deep Story characters available in Mystic Messenger. He is a hacker who can quickly find information on anything he can get his hands on." - Mystic Messenger

That is an excerpt from the official wiki page on that character. I've been using this character as a bit of a person on the internet! I am a programmer of 9 years and musician of 12. I like to relax and game with friends and love a good anime from time to time.

Why use this character? Well it's nothing formal. I do not have any rights to this character but many in my social group who I work and code for/with told me that our personalities are simmilar and I picked him up! Who doesn't like a character in something they can relate too!
